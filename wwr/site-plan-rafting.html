Scenario questions:

1- What type of experience do you offer for beginners?

Answer: We offer a variety of beginner-friendly rafting trips that are perfect for first-timers. These trips are led by experienced guides who will provide thorough safety instructions and help participants navigate the rapids.

2- Can you accommodate large groups or corporate events?

Answer: Yes, we can accommodate large groups and corporate events. We offer custom packages for groups of all sizes and can tailor the experience to meet the specific needs of each group.

3- What is the level of difficulty for your most challenging rafting trip?

Answer: Our most challenging rafting trip is rated as a Class V, which is considered extremely difficult and requires expert-level skills. This trip is only recommended for experienced rafters who are seeking an adrenaline-pumping adventure.
